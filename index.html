About



What is CrunchyCarrots?

CrunchyCarrots is a YouTube Channel that started late April of 2021.
Ever since then, a variety of videos and websites have been published.
CrunchyCarrots features miniature cooking for small pets and DIY tutorials.
As CrunchyCarrots continues to grow, we support feedback from all users.

Who are the stars of CrunchyCarrots?

Who are the stars of CrunchyCarrots? CrunchyCarrots cooks for Guinea Pigs.
The two main Guinea Pigs of the channel are Oliver & Theodore (images below).
Oliver & Theodore are taste-testers for the food that CrunchyCarrots makes.
All information about Oliver & Theodore can be found on the "Pigs" page.
